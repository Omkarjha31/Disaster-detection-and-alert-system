📢 Disaster Awareness & Safety

Learn about different natural disasters and how to stay safe.

🌍 Earthquake

Safety Measures: Drop, Cover, Hold. Stay away from windows and heavy objects.

🌊 Flood

Move to higher ground. Avoid floodwaters. Stay updated with official alerts.

🔥 Forest Fire

Evacuate immediately. Cover nose/mouth with a damp cloth.

🏔️ Landslide

Stay away from slopes and cliffs. Listen for warning signs.
